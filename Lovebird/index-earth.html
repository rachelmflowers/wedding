The Bride

Lori Johansson

Lori Johansson grew up in the Bay Area, spending much of her childhood in Palo Alto and Cupertino. She studied at Harvard and worked in New York for two years before going to get her MBA at INSEAD in Fontainebleau. Today, she works as an associate at Washburne Ventures in London.

The Groom

Keet Reeves

Keet Reeves was born in Washington and spent much of his life in Milan, moving to Paris at the age of 18 to study fine art at the École des Beaux-Arts. His work is known for blurring the line between technology and art and has been selected for display at exhibitions in Paris, Shanghai, and New York.

We met in 2009, at a backpacker hostel in Amsterdam. We hit it off immediately, bonding over our love of Gauguin and jazz. We had a magical time together in France during Lori's first year at grad school, and we both knew it was time. We were engaged in March 2014, and now we're getting married!
